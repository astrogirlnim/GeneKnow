Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy-First Commitment

GeneKnow is built on the principle of privacy-first genomic analysis. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and software.

Information We Collect

Website Usage

When you visit our website, we may collect standard web analytics information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • IP address (anonymized)

Download Information

We may track download statistics to understand which platforms are most popular and to improve our service.

Information We DON'T Collect

We never collect, store, or transmit your genetic data. All genomic analysis happens locally on your device. Your genetic information never leaves your computer.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Understand usage patterns
  • Provide technical support
  • Comply with legal requirements

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we collect. However, the most important security feature is that your genetic data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
